About SHA-2 Family

SHA-224

Produces a 224-bit (28-byte) hash value, typically rendered as 56 hexadecimal digits.

SHA-256

Produces a 256-bit (32-byte) hash value, typically rendered as 64 hexadecimal digits.

SHA-384

Produces a 384-bit (48-byte) hash value, typically rendered as 96 hexadecimal digits.

SHA-512

Produces a 512-bit (64-byte) hash value, typically rendered as 128 hexadecimal digits.
